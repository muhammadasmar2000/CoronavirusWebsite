About this service

Our service allows you to search coronavirus information by country name. Coronavirus statistics include: daily cases, total cases, daily deaths, total deaths, country population, those who have recovered, those in critical condition, and the amount of people that have been tested. Our service also gives relevant news information relating to coronavirus that you can click on a link and read an article.
